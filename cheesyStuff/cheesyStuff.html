Ingredients
* Cabbage
* Scallions
* 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
* ¾ teaspoon sea salt
* 3 eggs, beaten
* Extra-virgin olive oil
* Mayo
* Sriracha sauce
* Sesame seeds
* Soy sauce
Methods
1. Shred cabbage with a mandolin as thin as possible until you get 3 fully packed cups.
2. Chop scallions finely until you get 1 ¼ cups.
3. In a large bowl, combine the cabbage, scallions, panko, and salt.
4. Mix the eggs in the bowl gently. (Note: the mixture will be very loose and cabbagey, not like a flour pancake batter. If it's very dry, let it sit for 10 minutes).
5. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Brush the skillet with olive oil and use a ¼ measuring cup to scoop the cabbage mixture into the skillet. (It's ok if it doesn't seem cohesive, it'll bind together as the egg cooks).
6. Flatten gently with a spatula so that the mixture is about 1/2 inch thick. Cook 3 minutes per side, or until browned, turning the heat to low as needed.
7. Mix mayo with sriracha to taste.
8. To serve, drizzle the okonomiyaki with the sriracha mayo and top with sesame seeds and soy sauce.
9. If desired, sprinkle with slices or nori sheets, pickled ginger and microgreens.
Ingredients
* 2 cans of crushed tomatoes (796 ml)
* 1 large onion
* 2 garlic cloves
* 2-3 celery stalks
* 1 cup of mushrooms
* 1-2 bell pepper
* ½ pepperoni
* 1½ cup of parmesan cheese
* 1½ kg of ground meat
* 2 eggs
* Olive oil
* Parsley, Basil
* ¼ to ½ cup of Italian spices
* 2-3 bay leaves
* Salt and Pepper, to taste
* Cayenne pepper, to taste
* Sugar, to taste
Methods
1. Finely dice the onion, the bell peppers and the pepperoni.
2. Finely slice the garlic, the celery and the mushrooms.
3. Beat the eggs in a small bowl for later use.
4. In a large pot, cook the onion with olive oil and add the bay leaves.
5. Add all the vegetables and keep mixing to avoid burning them.
6. Add the garlic, crushed tomatoes, Italian spices, basil, sugar, cayenne pepper, pepperoni, salt and pepper.
7. Reduce the temperature to simmer and put a lid on it while you prepare the meat.
8. In a large bol, mix with your hand the ground meat, beaten eggs, parsley, Italian spices, ½ cup of parmesan cheese, salt and pepper.
9. Add the meat mix to the pot while stirring.
10. Let the sauce simmer for 1 hour while covered.
11. Make sure to stir the pot regularly.
12. After the hour, you should add an extra cup of Parmesan cheese.
13. Now you can adjust the flavour to your liking by adding more salt, pepper, cayenne pepper, sugar until you are happy.

Ingredients
* 4 large chicken breasts
* salt & pepper
* 1½ teaspoon of garlic powder
* ¼ teaspoon of paprika
* 1 cup broccoli florets
* ½ cup red bell pepper
* 1 cup mild cheddar cheese
* 1 tablespoon of Mayo
* 2 tablespoon of olive oil
Methods
1. Finely chop the broccoli, finely dice the bell pepper and shred the cheese.
2. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
3. Season both sides of the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, paprika and ½ tsp of the garlic powder.
4. Use a sharp knife to cut a slice through the middle of the chicken breasts, but not all the way through, creating a pocket for the filling.
5. In a bowl combine the broccoli, bell pepper, cheese, mayo, remaining garlic powder and salt and pepper to taste.
6. Mix until combined, then divide the mixture between the chicken breasts, using a toothpick to secure if necessary.
7. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and add the olive oil.
8. Sear the chicken for 3 to 4 minutes on each side.
9. Transfer the chicken to a container that goes into the oven. Cover the container with foil.
10. Bake in preheated oven for 15-17 minutes or until chicken is cooked.
11. Let rest, covered for 5 minutes before enjoying.
Ingredients
* 1.5 kg pork shoulder or tenderloin
* 4 to 5 cups of chicken stock
* 4 garlic cloves
* 1 medium onion
* 1 teaspoon cumin
* 1 teaspoon cinnamon powder or 1 cinnamon stick
* 2 bay leaves
* 1 tablespoon soy sauce
* 1 can of evaporated milk
* Juice from 1 fresh orange
* Salt
* Ground black pepper
Methods
1. Crush the garlic and roughly chop the onion.
2. Cut pork into large cubes.
3. Place pork shoulder cubes and fat in a large bowl.
4. Season very generously with salt and ground black pepper.
5. Add crushed garlic cloves, chopped onion, cumin, cinnamon, bay leaves, soy sauce, evaporated milk and orange juice.
6. Mix well and let the meat marinate for 20 minutes.
7. Brown meat in the pressure cooker. Leave only a short period of time per side only to sear the meat.
8. Put all the seared meat with the marinade in the instant pot or pressure cooker.
9. Pour chicken stock.
10. Close lid and pressure cook at high pressure for 30 minutes with 15 minutes natural release.
11. Release the remaining pressure after 15 minutes and open the lid carefully.
12. Transfer the pork shoulder cubes onto a baking tray. Taste and see if the pork cubes are salty enough. If the pork cubes are not salty enough, add more salt into the cooking liquid.
13. Drizzle some of the cooking liquid on to each pork piece. The cooking liquid should be mostly fat. If not, mix ¼ cup (63ml) cooking liquid with 2 tbsp (30ml) of vegetable oil and drizzle over each piece of pork.
14. Place pork shoulder cubes under a broiler or the top rack of a preheated 450°F oven until the surface is crisped. Roughly 8 – 10 minutes.
15. Flip the pork shoulder cubes, drizzle each piece with more cooking liquid and place in the oven again until the surface is crisped.
16. Serve with tortillas, sauce, lemon and pico de gallo.
Ingredients
* 1 whole chicken
* 2 red peppers
* 2 mangos
* ½ bag of frozen edamame
* 1 bunch of spinach
* 1 small pack of pasta
* 1 can of coco milk
* 1 can of baby sweetcorn
* ½ cup of mango sauce or puree
* Green onion
* Juice of one lemon
* ½ teaspoon ginger
* Salt
* Ground black pepper
Methods
1. Cook and shred the chicken.
2. Put oil in a big pan and fry chicken until brown.
3. Add the spinach, red pepper cut in cubes, sweetcorn and edamame.
4. In a bowl, mix the coco milk, the mango sauce and the ginger.
5. Add the coco/mango mix to the chicken and adjust with water to cover.
6. Add the pack of pasta and mix.
7. Add salt and black pepper to taste.
8. Slow cook the mix for 3 hours, mixing from time to time.
9. Add the mango, the chopped green onion and the lemon juice.
10. Adjust the salt and black pepper to taste.
Ingredients
* 2 cans of beans (any type is fine)
* ½ can chipotle
* 1 onion
* Oil
* Sour cream
* Cheese
* Green onion
* Salt
Methods
1. Chop the onion in small cubes.
2. In a pan, warm oil and add the onion until caramelized.
3. Rinse beans with water using a strainer. This step is important to remove the excess salt from the canned beans.
4. Add beans in the pan with onion and let them warm for a couple of minutes.
5. Using a masher, mash the beans a little and let them mix with the onions.
6. Put the semi-mashed beans and onions in the blender with the ½ can of chipotle and blend them all together.
7. Add a bit more oil in a pan, warm it and add the blended mix to reheat. Adjust salt as needed.
8. Shred cheese.
9. Cut green onion
10. To serve, add beans with top of sour cream, cheese, and green onion.
Ingredients
* 1 can of jalapeño in vinegar (200 ml)
* 1 tomato
* Sour cream
* 1 bag of chips (preferably natural and thick)
Methods
1. Cut tomato in 4.
2. In blender, add cut tomato and the full content of the jalapeño can (including vinegar).
3. Blend the ingredients until only small pieces are visible.
4. To serve, put the bag of chips in a bowl, add jalapeño sauce and adjust the spice with sour cream.
5. Eat with a spoon if you don't want to dirty your hands.
